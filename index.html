I am an Assistant Professor at the University of AlQadisiyah and a Visiting Fellow at Bournemouth University. My research focuses on artificial intelligence, speech technology, and forensic voice comparison. I work on projects that integrate machine learning, deep learning, and signal processing for real-world forensic and communication systems.

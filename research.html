Research Areas

  • Forensic voice comparison and speaker verification

    Work on deep speaker embeddings, x-vector, ECAPA-TDNN, WavLM, and evaluation of voices under challenging conditions.

  • Speech deepfake detection

    Research on synthetic speech detection, anti-spoofing, robustness of speaker verification, and analysis of deepfake impact on forensic decisions.

  • Speech and signal processing

    Feature extraction, voice analysis, classification methods, and development of machine learning systems for acoustic tasks.

  • Medical image processing

    Work on MRI brain tumor analysis, classification, and federated learning environments for medical applications.

  • Artificial intelligence

    Machine learning, deep learning, and intelligent data analysis across speech, security, and healthcare domains.
